# Journal-Neutral Checklist

- [ ] Title and abstract avoid clinical, regulatory, and certification claims.
- [ ] Contribution list is limited to manifest profile, validator, synthetic
      examples, golden receipts, reproducibility capsule, and release asset
      verification.
- [ ] References in `paper/references-to-check.md` are human-reviewed.
- [ ] DOI wording says DOI-ready only and does not claim an assigned DOI.
- [ ] Device UID is not treated as UDI-DI.
- [ ] Offline registry fixture first is stated.
- [ ] Live openFDA is explicit opt-in.
- [ ] FDO-style mapping is not described as official FDO implementation.
- [ ] Private suite and private service material are excluded from public paper
      data availability.
