# Reviewer FAQ

## Is this clinical validation?

No. It is not clinical validation.

## Is this regulatory approval?

No. It is not regulatory approval.

## Does this replace PACS/VNA?

No. It only validates a small evidence packet.

## Does this use real patient data?

No. Public examples are synthetic and no PHI is allowed.

## What does registry resolution mean?

It means a declared UDI-DI was matched to an offline fixture or explicit live
lookup result. It is not medical decision support.

## Why Device UID is not UDI-DI?

Device UID is a DICOM equipment UID. UDI-DI is the fixed device identifier
portion of a regulatory UDI.

## What is public vs private?

Public materials include the profile, schema, examples, validator, API, tests,
and demo. Private partner cases and vendor rules belong in a separate controlled
repository.
