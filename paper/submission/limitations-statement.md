# Limitations Statement

- Public examples are synthetic and do not represent field deployment.
- Raw DICOM files are not included.
- Device UID is not UDI-DI.
- Offline registry fixture review is the default.
- Live openFDA lookup is explicit opt-in.
- FDO-style mapping is metadata alignment only, not official FDO implementation.
- The software does not provide clinical validation, regulatory approval,
  certification, diagnosis, safety assurance, or hospital deployment evidence.
