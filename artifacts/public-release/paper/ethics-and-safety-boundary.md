# Ethics And Safety Boundary

The public validator is designed for software review of synthetic evidence
fixtures. It should not be used as a medical decision system, a diagnosis tool,
a regulatory approval mechanism, a certification authority, or a hospital
deployment assessment.

Required safe-language statements:

- Public examples are synthetic.
- No PHI is included.
- Raw DICOM files are not included.
- Device UID is not UDI-DI.
- Offline registry fixture review is the default.
- Live openFDA lookup must be explicitly enabled.
- FDO-style mapping is not an official FDO implementation claim.
