# v0.7 Human Review Checklist

Use this checklist before any public paper submission, external technical
review, or archive-service deposition.

- Confirm the paper describes a minimal UDI-DICOM evidence manifest profile,
  deterministic reference validator, synthetic public examples, golden receipt
  regression, reproducibility capsule, and public release asset verification.
- Confirm the paper does not claim clinical validation, regulatory approval,
  certification, diagnosis, safety assurance, real hospital deployment, or DOI
  assignment.
- Confirm Device UID is not described as UDI-DI.
- Confirm offline registry fixtures are the default.
- Confirm live openFDA lookup is explicit opt-in.
- Confirm FDO-style mapping is described as metadata alignment only, not an
  official FDO implementation.
- Confirm private suite and private service repositories are not described as
  public datasets.
- Confirm all examples remain synthetic and contain no PHI or raw DICOM.
- Confirm reviewer feedback is routed through the v0.6 feedback intake before
  any scope change.
