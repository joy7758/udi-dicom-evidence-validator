# Limitations

- Public fixtures are synthetic and do not represent field deployment.
- The repository does not include raw DICOM files.
- The validator does not process PHI.
- Registry review defaults to offline fixtures.
- Live openFDA lookup is explicit opt-in and is not required for public tests.
- FDO-style mapping is descriptive metadata, not an official FDO
  implementation.
- Device UID is not UDI-DI.
- The project does not provide clinical validation, regulatory approval,
  certification, diagnosis, safety assurance, or hospital deployment evidence.
