# Data Availability

All public examples are synthetic JSON fixtures under `examples/public/`.
Generated public outputs are under `tests/golden/`, `artifacts/`, and
`demo/portable-ultrasound/artifacts_v0.2/`.

No PHI, raw DICOM files, real patient records, real hospital data, real customer
data, or private conformance cases are included in the public repository.

Private partner review and service workflows are intentionally kept outside the
public validator repository.
