# Cooperation Brief

Purpose: review whether de-identified DICOM equipment metadata and UDI evidence
can be packaged into a deterministic evidence receipt.

Partner inputs: de-identified metadata extracts, declared UDI evidence, registry
lookup context, and reviewable artifact summaries. No PHI or original clinical
reports should be sent.

Outputs: validation receipts, Markdown reports, issue summaries, and remediation
recommendations. Outputs are not clinical validation, not regulatory approval,
and not certification.

After NDA, a partner pack can be prepared through the private conformance suite.
